## SCRIPT-03 — Testimonial format (run only with real learners)
**Concept (one line):** A real learner shows the actual asset they built and states the hours it saves.

| Timecode | Voiceover / on-screen text | Visual / shot |
|----------|----------------------------|---------------|
| 0:00–0:06 | "[REAL_LEARNER_QUOTE — the pain before]" | Learner on camera in their workspace, or screen-share intro. |
| 0:06–0:18 | "[What they built — in their words]" | [SCREENSHOT/SCREEN-RECORD: their actual capstone asset running] |
| 0:18–0:28 | "[The measured result — '[METRIC] hours/week back']" | On-screen the real number; their certificate. |
| 0:28–0:35 | "Build your own. Design your course." | Brand end-card + CTA. |

**Rules:** Do **not** produce this with fabricated quotes or numbers. Capture real ones at the capstone (the course already measures ROI and issues a verifiable certificate — that's your built-in testimonial engine). Get written consent. Keep claims to what the learner actually achieved.

**Captions (sound-off):** Subtitle the learner verbatim; overlay the metric as a bold number.

### Production notes
- Keep the warm, grounded look (amber/clay on near-black) consistent with the app and image set — no stock "tech blue."
- Real product screenshots > animations. The app's screens are the strongest visual proof.
- One CTA per video: `Design my course`. Don't dilute with multiple asks.
