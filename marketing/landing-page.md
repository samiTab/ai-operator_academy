## 4. How it works
A clear five-step path — most learners finish in **4–8 hours**, spread however suits them:

1. **Enroll** — answer 8 short questions about your role, goal, and the one task you'd love to hand off.
2. **Diagnose** — the academy reads your answers and identifies your lead track and the right depth for you.
3. **Personalize** — you get a custom path: the right modules, in the right order, with examples from *your* industry. Skip what you don't need.
4. **Learn by doing** — every lesson ends with a hands-on task you do in a live Claude practice sandbox, with instant, specific feedback.
5. **Ship & certify** — you build a real capstone asset (an automation, tool, or workflow), measure the time it saves, and earn a verifiable certificate that shows what you built.

No. Most of the course needs zero code. The Builder track introduces Claude Code in plain English — you describe what you want and it writes it; you stay in control. Non-technical learners can skip or lightly sample it.

**How long does it take?**
Most learners finish in 4–8 hours total, at their own pace. The path is right-sized to the time you tell us you have.

**Is the certificate accredited?**
No — and we say so plainly. It's a verifiable demonstration of practical, hands-on capability (with a real asset you built and measured ROI), not an academic credential. That's what makes it useful to show.

**What happens when Claude changes / adds features?**
The tools evolve fast, so we teach the durable concepts and mark anything UI-specific as "check the latest docs." Pro/Cohort members get a "living curriculum" — new lessons appear as capabilities ship.

**Is my data safe?**
You practice with examples you choose, and every lesson includes a privacy note. We teach you what's safe to put into AI and what isn't — data handling is part of the curriculum, not an afterthought.
