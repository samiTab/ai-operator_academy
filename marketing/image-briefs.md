### Usage notes
- Keep text overlays short; let the headline do the work.
- For any mockup that represents the actual product, prefer a **real screenshot** (`[SCREENSHOT: …]`) over a generated approximation so the UI is accurate.
- Maintain the warm, grounded palette across every asset — it's the brand's differentiator from cold "tech blue" AI marketing.
