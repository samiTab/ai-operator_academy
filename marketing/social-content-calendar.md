## Week 1

### Day 1 — Quick tip (value)
- **Hook:** Most people use AI like a search box. Here's the 5-line tweak that makes it act like a teammate instead.
- **Body:** Don't ask "how do I write a follow-up email?" Brief it like a new hire:
  > You're my assistant at a [business]. Context: [situation]. The job: [the output I want]. What "good" looks like: [format, tone, length]. Ask me up to 2 questions first, then do it.
  Same AI. Completely different result — you get a finished draft, not a list of tips.
- **CTA:** Save this for your next email. Want a whole course built around your real tasks? Link in comments.

- **CTA:** Try it on the next thing you ask AI and reply with what changed.
